My Education Journey

Here's a glimpse of my education path from the beginning until now.

Kindergarten

I begin my early education at Tadika Kemas when I was around four years old. It was where I learned the basics od reading,writing, and counting. I still remember the colourful classrooms, and how I start to communicate with other kids

Primary School

Sekolah Kebangsaan Paya Pulai | 2012-2017

I begin my early education at Sekolah Kebangsaan Paya Pulai. Those six years were full of fun memories, from joining school events and making new friends to learning important life values.

Secondary School

Sekolah Sukan Negeri Pahang, Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Seberang Temerloh | 2018-2022

I continue my studies at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Seberang Temerloh (Sports School). My secondary school years taught me discipline, and teamwork. Besides, I was chosen as a target student for the SPM examination and also served as the Head of Pembimbing Rakan Sebaya (PRS) at my school

Diploma Journey

Currently pursuing my Diploma in Information Management (CDIM110), where this course has taugt me the importance of organizing,and managing information effectively in the digital era. It also helps me to develop critical thinking, and communication skills
